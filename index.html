About the Community

The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) at IIIT Kalyani is a vibrant student-led community that fosters learning and collaboration in technology. It empowers students to develop skills in areas like web development, app development, machine learning, and more through workshops, sessions, and projects. GDSC IIIT Kalyani encourages innovation, teamwork, and leadership to solve real-world challenges.

Gen AI Study Jams

Gen AI Study Jams are workshops or events organized by Google to help students learn about Generative AI technology. Participants get to dive into the basics of Generative AI and then apply their learning by building projects using Google Cloud services. It's a hands-on opportunity for students to explore this exciting field of AI and develop practical skills in using AI tools and platforms.
